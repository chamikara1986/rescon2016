About the Event

The PGIS Research Congress is an annual research conference organized by the Postgraduate Institute of Science. With investigators from across Sri Lanka and abroad presenting the cutting edge of science and technology, the Research Congress provides an exciting opportunity to share knowledge and network with contemporary standard bearers of research.

Important Dates

Presentations will be held on five broad themes

  • Submission of Abstracts: 15th February - 20th April 2016

  • Normal Registration: 7th September - 30th September 2016
